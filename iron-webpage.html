The Iron Giant

About The Iron Giant

The Iron Giant is a sentient mechanical from outer space. His arrival to Earth was troubling to the humans nearby, except for one boy. Hogarth Hughes was welcoming to the Giant and became friends very quickly. The Iron Giant was later brutally attacked by the US Military.

The Iron Giant meeting Hogarth Hughes in the woods.

Here are 3 fun facts!

  1. His story was originally written under 'The Iron Man' by Ted Hughes.
  2. It is believed, in the novelisation, that he has a wife, The Iron Woman, somewhere in space.
  3. He has deep feelings that often combat his cool and calculating logic.
